Eco Touch - A new journey of Viet Straw to go global

Joining forces with EcoSmart Solutions, ECO Touch embarks on an exhilarating journey alongside VietStraw! As Vietnam's trailblazer in the green movement, VietStraw has introduced rice straws - an eco-friendly alternative that perfectly aligns with the commitment to sustainability. Empowering consumers and businesses to switch from plastic to biodegradable rice straws, ECO Touch aims to expand its impact to international markets.

Welcome to ECO Touch - an expedition brimming with promise and breakthroughs in the UK and European market. As the pioneer and creator of VietStraw in Vietnam, ECO Touch proudly presents biodegradable rice straws - an impressive eco-conscious product captivating Vietnamese consumers.

The mission revolves around building a greener and cleaner Vietnam through eco-friendly solutions, with rice straws as a significant step in the journey. Utilizing rice - a bountiful natural resource in Vietnam - as the primary material, ECO Touch ensures its products are environmentally benign, making them the intelligent and responsible choice for the community.

Despite early challenges, ECO Touch's unwavering passion and perseverance propelled the dream of creating a positive environmental impact and enhancing people's lives.

A pivotal milestone was the acquisition of the first rice straw production machine, marking the beginning of an eco-friendly production process and ensuring product excellence. ECO Touch's first rice straws emerged, carrying a powerful message of sustainability and significance for the community.

First partners, believers in ECO Touch, played a pivotal role in inspiring continuous improvement and delivering unparalleled value to customers, contributing to a greener Vietnam

With unwavering support from customers and partners, ECO Touch expands into international markets, championing sustainability in the UK and European countries. Standing firm, ECO Touch envisions a brighter future, orchestrating a beautiful symphony for the environment worldwide!
